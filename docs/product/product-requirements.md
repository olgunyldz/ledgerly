# Product Requirements

## Product statement

Ledgerly helps UK taxpayers keep clean records, understand and prepare their tax information in their own language, and preserve official English tax terminology where it matters.

## Product identity

- **Name:** Ledgerly
- **Category:** Multi-language UK tax record and preparation assistant
- **Core promise:** Keep clear records, understand your tax position and prepare accountant-ready summaries.
- **Trust stance:** Ledgerly explains and organises; deterministic rules calculate; high-risk cases escalate to accountant review.

## MVP audience

- UK-based self-employed workers
- Gig economy drivers and couriers
- Freelancers and contractors
- Small landlords
- Users who prefer non-native-English tax explanations

## MVP outcomes

Users should be able to:

1. Select language and create a basic tax profile.
2. Add income and expenses for a tax year.
3. Upload receipts or documents for extraction review.
4. Ask tax questions in their chosen language.
5. See a cautious tax estimate.
6. Export an accountant-ready summary.

## Out of scope for MVP

- Direct HMRC submission
- Full open banking integration
- VAT returns
- Corporation Tax
- Payroll
- Regulated tax advice without professional review

## Trust requirements

- Show calculation assumptions.
- Show whether a value is user-entered, extracted or estimated.
- Show source references for tax explanations.
- Clearly label estimates as estimates.
- Make accountant review easy for high-risk cases.
