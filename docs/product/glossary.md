# UK Tax Glossary

Use this glossary for multi-language explanations. Keep official English terms visible.

| Key | English term | Turkish explanation | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| allowable_expense | Allowable expense | Vergiden düşülebilen iş gideri | Avoid saying every business cost is automatically allowable. |
| personal_allowance | Personal Allowance | Vergisiz kişisel gelir hakkı | Can be reduced at higher incomes. |
| self_assessment | Self Assessment | Kişisel vergi beyanı sistemi | Official HMRC process. |
| payment_on_account | Payment on Account | Gelecek yıl vergisi için avans ödeme | Often surprises first-time users. |
| utr | Unique Taxpayer Reference | Kişiye özel vergi referans numarası | Sensitive identifier. |
| national_insurance | National Insurance | Ulusal sigorta primi | Not the same as Income Tax. |
| trading_allowance | Trading Allowance | Küçük ticari gelirler için yıllık muafiyet | Must be explained carefully with expenses interaction. |
| basis_period | Basis Period | Vergilendirilecek dönem | Advanced topic. |
| mileage_allowance | Mileage allowance | İş amaçlı yol için mil başına gider yöntemi | Different from actual-cost method. |
