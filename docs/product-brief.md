# Product Brief

## Name

Ledgerly

## One-liner

A calm, multi-language UK tax assistant that helps users keep clean records, understand what matters and prepare accountant-ready tax summaries.

## Positioning

Ledgerly turns everyday income, expenses and documents into understandable UK tax records. It is not an aggressive filing tool or a black-box tax calculator; it is a careful assistant that helps users stay organised, understand official terms and know when to ask an accountant.

## Brand promise

Keep clear records. Understand your tax. Share a clean pack with your accountant.

## Target users

- UK self-employed workers
- Gig economy drivers and couriers
- Freelancers and contractors
- Small landlords
- Multilingual users who need tax explanations in their own language

## Differentiation

Most tax tools assume the user is comfortable with English tax terminology. Ledgerly explains UK tax in the user's language, keeps official English terms visible, and creates accountant-ready summaries without pretending uncertain tax questions are simple.

## MVP promise

“Understand what to record, estimate what to set aside, and prepare a clean pack for your accountant.”

## Brand personality

- Calm, practical and precise.
- Helpful without sounding like an adviser making final decisions.
- Professional enough for tax records, simple enough for first-time self-employed users.
- Multilingual by default, with official English tax terms preserved where they matter.

## Strategic wedge

Start with English + Turkish for self-employed users, then expand to Arabic, Urdu, Polish, Romanian, Bulgarian and Hindi.
